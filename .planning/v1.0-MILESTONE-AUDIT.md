# Milestone v1.0 Audit Report

**Milestone:** UI/UX Redesign

## Executive Summary

Phases 1-4 are fully verified and correctly wired. The foundation layer (design tokens, theme switching, component library, navigation shell) is solid. However, the milestone defines 8 phases and only 4 have been planned and executed. Phases 5-8 (Dashboard, Browse, Update/Reports, Animations) carry 18 of the 25 v1 requirements and have not been started.

The completed phases have strong cross-phase integration (16/18 exports wired correctly). Two integration notes: Phase 3's SharedUI components are not yet consumed by App views (expected — phases 5-7 will wire them), and the `motionAnimation()` reduce-motion extension is orphaned (addressable in Phase 8 polish).
